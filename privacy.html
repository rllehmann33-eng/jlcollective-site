Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

This policy describes how JL Collective LLC ("JL Collective," "we," "us") handles information on this website and in the course of providing our services. We have tried to write it in plain language, because that is how we would want it written for us.

This website

This website does not set cookies, does not run analytics, and does not use advertising trackers. We do not collect personal information from visitors who are simply browsing.

Web fonts on this site are served by Google Fonts. When your browser requests those font files, Google receives the technical details of that request (such as your IP address) under its own privacy policy.

If you contact us

If you email us, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include. We use it to respond to you and to keep a record of our correspondence. We do not sell it, rent it, or add you to marketing lists you did not ask for.

Client services

When we provide marketing or software services to a business, we may access that business's advertising accounts and related data through platform APIs (for example, Meta's marketing APIs) — always with the business's explicit authorization, and only to provide the services they engaged us for. In that work:

  • We access only the data needed to plan, monitor, report on, and manage the client's own marketing.
  • We do not sell client data or use one client's data for another client's benefit.
  • Actions that affect a client's accounts are reviewed and approved by an authorized person before they are taken.
  • Clients may revoke our access at any time through the relevant platform's business settings (for example, Meta Business Manager).

Data retention and deletion

We keep service records only as long as needed to provide the services, meet our legal and accounting obligations, and maintain an audit history of approved actions. To request deletion of data we hold about you or your business, email hello@jlcollective.co with the subject line "Data deletion request." We will confirm receipt, and we will act on verified requests within 30 days unless the law requires us to retain something, in which case we will tell you what and why.

Security

We use access controls, credential isolation, and audit logging in the systems we operate. No system is perfectly secure, but we build ours so that access is limited, recorded, and revocable.

Children

Our website and services are for businesses and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we change this policy, we will update it here with a new effective date. Material changes to how we handle client data are communicated to clients directly.
